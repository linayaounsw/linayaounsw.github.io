Group Leader

  • Lina YaoSchool of Computer Science and Engineering, UNSW
  • General Introduction

    Our group leader Lina is currently a lecturer @ School of Computer Science and Engineering , The University of New South Wales (UNSW), Australia. She is ARC Discovery Early Career Researcher Award (DECRA) Fellow between 2016-2018 (awarded in 2015).
    The whole group research interest lies in Internet of Things Analytics/Cyber-Physical Systems, Data Mining, Pervasive and Ubiquitous Computing, Ambient Intelligence and Service Computing.
