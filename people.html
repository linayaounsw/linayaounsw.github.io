Group leader

Lina Yao


Our group leader Lina is currently a senior lecturer at School of Computer Science and Engineering, The University of New South Wales (UNSW), Australia. She is ARC Discovery Early Career Researcher Award (DECRA) Fellow between 2016-2018 (awarded in 2015).
The whole group research interest lies in Internet of Things Analytics/Cyber-Physical Systems, Data Mining,Brain Computer Interface, Pervasive and Ubiquitous Computing, Ambient Intelligence and Service Computing.
