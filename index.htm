Welcome to InsData!

InsData is dedicated on getting inspriations from data via designing and developing sound and robust data management and analysis techniques and systems. We work on a wide spectrum of problems across the areas of data mining, machine learning and pervasive intelligence, especially those that arise in the application domains of Internet of Things, Cyber-Physical Systems, information filtering and recommending, and human activity recognition and modeling.
